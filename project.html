Scientific News Channel Project

A flagship initiative developed alongside Here News — applying the same principles of integrity, verification, and respect for original sources to scientific knowledge, with a focus on Arabic heritage and modern education.

Support the mission Follow the project

What this is

A scientific media and knowledge initiative aimed at Arabic-speaking youth — bridging ancient scientific heritage with modern science and responsible technology.

The long-term vision includes a scientific news channel, a learning portal, and a searchable manuscript ecosystem.

Why now

High-quality scientific content in Arabic is scarce and often degraded through layers of translation. Many classical Arabic scientific manuscripts remain inaccessible, undigitized, or not searchable — leaving centuries of knowledge trapped and underused.

The gap we’re solving

  • Loss of meaning in translation: successive translations blur original intent and terminology.
  • Sparse content & AI bias: limited high-quality Arabic datasets reduce accuracy and increase bias.
  • Heritage access barriers: valuable manuscripts remain inaccessible or difficult to interpret.
  • Non-searchable archives: many scans are image-only, preventing discovery and research.

What this project will build

Digitized manuscripts Searchable archive Standardized terminology Youth media channel Curated datasets Expert review process

Mission

Protect, digitize, and revive original Arabic scientific manuscripts and present scientific knowledge in Arabic with accuracy, depth, and cultural integrity — using modern technology responsibly.

Vision

A future where Arabic is a first-class scientific language, youth learn from authentic sources, and modern systems — including AI — serve Arabic speakers accurately because they are grounded in high-quality, verified data.

How supporters help

Donations enable early foundation work needed to build a sustainable initiative:

  • Research, sourcing, and digitization workflows
  • Content production with expert review
  • Archive infrastructure (hosting, tooling, organization)
  • Long-term development without compromising integrity

How it connects to Here News

Here News is the broader platform for verified public-interest reporting. The Scientific News Channel Project is a flagship vertical developed alongside it — focused on scientific truth, education, and original sources in Arabic.
