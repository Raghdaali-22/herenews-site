Scientific News Channel Project

Protecting and growing Arabic scientific knowledge by reviving original manuscripts, improving access to rigorous science in Arabic, and building a future-ready learning and media ecosystem.

Support the mission Follow the project

The problem

High-quality scientific content in Arabic is scarce and often distorted through layers of translation. Many classical Arabic scientific manuscripts remain inaccessible, undigitized, or not searchable — leaving centuries of knowledge trapped and unusable.

The opportunity

By digitizing sources, standardizing terminology, and producing modern scientific media in Arabic, we can restore continuity — from classical scholarship to modern research and responsible AI.

What this project will build

Digitized manuscripts Searchable archive Arabic terminology Youth media channel Curated datasets

This initiative sits under Here News — the broader platform for science, knowledge, and truth in Arabic.

What is Here News?

Mission

Protect, digitize, and revive original Arabic scientific manuscripts and present scientific knowledge in Arabic with accuracy, depth, and cultural integrity — using modern technology responsibly.

Vision

A future where Arabic is a first-class scientific language, youth learn from authentic sources, and AI systems serve Arabic speakers accurately because they are trained on high-quality, culturally grounded data.

How supporters help

Donations enable the early foundation work needed to build a sustainable project:

  • Research and manuscript digitization initiatives
  • Content production with expert review
  • Technical infrastructure (hosting, tools, archiving workflows)
  • Long-term development without compromising editorial independence
