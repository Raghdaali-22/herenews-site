Science, knowledge, and truth — built for the Arabic-speaking world.

Here News is an independent platform in development. The goal is to publish rigorous, culturally grounded scientific content, reconnect learners with original sources, and support responsible technology that preserves meaning — not distortion.

Explore our first initiative Follow the story

Mission

Build a credible, accessible science-first media platform in Arabic that prioritizes accuracy, depth, and original context — free from shallow translation and trend-driven content.

Vision

A future where Arabic is a first-class language for scientific thinking, learning, and discovery — connected to both classical heritage and modern research.

What is Here News?

A long-term knowledge platform that bridges heritage, modern science, and future technology. Not built for clicks — built for continuity.
