What is Here News?

Here News is designed for independent journalism — local reporters, citizen journalists, photographers, and videographers who witness events and can report with proof.

Readers (and partner outlets) get authentic, real-time local stories from across the world — with a way to support journalists directly.

Think of it as a blend of Associated Press + Uber + Patreon — built for modern, decentralized reporting.

How it works

  • Journalists publish geo-tagged stories with media proof.
  • Contributor profiles build reputation through verification and peer validation.
  • Optional AI-assisted checks flag inconsistencies or plagiarism.
  • Readers can support reporters directly; outlets can license exclusives.

Mission

Build a trusted global platform that elevates local voices, protects credibility in reporting, and enables sustainable income for independent journalists.

Vision

A world where real-time local journalism is easier to verify, easier to discover, and fairer to the people who create it — while restoring public trust in what is true.

Coming soon, built responsibly

Here News is currently in the research and early development phase. The focus is on defining verification standards, designing a fair payout model, and preparing pilot regions with a small group of trusted contributors.

Early supporters help fund development, verification workflows, and pilot testing — without compromising independence.

Our first flagship initiative

Alongside Here News, we are developing a specialized initiative focused on scientific knowledge in Arabic: the Scientific News Channel Project. It applies the same principles of integrity, verification, and respect for original sources — to science and heritage.
