Short Bio

Ali Gamal is a journalist and media professional with decades of experience covering major stories across the Middle East and internationally. Over the years, he has seen firsthand how scientific knowledge can be diluted through weak translation, shallow coverage, and loss of original context.

Here News and the Scientific News Channel Project are built to address that structural gap — not as a short campaign, but as a generational effort.

How the projects connect

Here News is the umbrella platform — a science-first media and knowledge hub.

The Scientific News Channel Project is the first flagship initiative — focused on protecting Arabic scientific heritage, building accessible learning content, and supporting responsible AI grounded in authentic Arabic corpora.

Guiding principles

These initiatives are grounded in three principles:

  • Accuracy: prioritize correct scientific meaning over virality.
  • Originality: reconnect learners with primary sources and authentic heritage.
  • Continuity: build systems that can last, expand, and be trusted.
